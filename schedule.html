Homework Grading Rubric
Points Attributes
6 Cadets present complete solution and answers are correct.
     
2 Cadets present complete solution and answers are incorrect.
1 Cadets present minimal work and answers are incorrect.
0 Answers are shown with no work.
Procedure:

1. Detailed grading comments will not be provided by instructor.

2. Cadets are responsible for reviewing approved solutions and finding mistakes.

3. Cadets may request a regrade upon correction of assignment. Corrections must be under separate cover with a new title (e.g., PS10 Corrections).

4. Your resubmission must identify what your error was and what you should have done. You must also repair the error and verify the correction.

5. Solutions will be posted by the instructor, and you may use the solutions to identify mistakes in your work. However, simply copying the instructor solution for the re-submission will not change your grade. You must make corrections to your work.

6. Notification of resubmission must be in writing via email within 24 hours of grade posting. Beyond that time, requests will not be allowed.
