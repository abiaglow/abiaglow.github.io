Homework Grading Policy and Rubric
Points Attributes
6 Cadets present complete solution and answers are correct.
     
2 Cadets present complete solution and answers are incorrect.
1 Cadets present minimal work and answers are incorrect.
0 Answers are shown with no work.
Procedure:

1. Detailed grading comments will not be provided by the instructor.

2. Cadets are responsible for reviewing approved solutions and finding mistakes.

3. Cadets may resubmit problem sets after grades have been posted. The instructor will notify the class when the assignment has been graded and the grades posted. Corrections must be under separate cover with a new title (e.g., PS10 Corrections). The instructor will find your resubmissions in Canvas. You are not required to email the instructor.

4. Your resubmission must identify what your error was and what you should have done. You must also repair the error and verify the correction.

5. Solutions will be posted by the instructor, and you may use those to identify mistakes in your work. However, simply copying the instructor solution for the resubmission will not change your grade. You must make corrections to your work and identify what you did wrong.

6. Resubmissions must be posted in Canvas within the suspense, generally 48 hours of notification of posting (weekends and holidays included). Beyond that time, resubmissions will not be allowed.

7. Original score must be greater than 1/6 to be eligible for a resubmission.

8. Missing pdf will result in a 2/6 per problem.

9. The maximum score for a resubmission is 5/6.
