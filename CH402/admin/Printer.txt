CONNECTING TO THE PRINTER:

1.  In the search field, type "\\print.westpoint.edu"
2.  Search for the printer named "WPPRCLS331>"
3.  Right-click and connect.
4.  Click "Next" when prompted.

ISSUES WITH PRINTING IN 331:

A recurring issue is that the name of the printer changes in firmware each time 
the printer gets a new IP address from DHCP.  Your instructor knows how to repair 
this issue, so if your print jobs fail, please report the issue ASAP.

Your failed job may prevent other cadets from printing from their computers.  
If you send something to the printer and it does not print out, the issue might
be with you and not the printer.  So if your print job fails, please be courteous 
and clear the print spooler.  

CLEARING THE PRINT SPOOLER:

1.  Click the Microsoft "Start" button on the lower left portion of your main monitor.
2.  Click "settings."
3.  On the left, click "Bluetooth & devices."
4.  Click "Printers & Scanners.
5.  Scroll down to the printer and click it.
6.  Open the print queue and delete any jobs associated with your name.
