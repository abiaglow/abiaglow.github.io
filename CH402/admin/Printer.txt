CONNECTING TO THE PRINTER:

1.  In the search field, type "\\print.westpoint.edu"
2.  Search for the printer named "WPPRCLS331>"
3.  Right-click and connect.
4.  Click "Next" when prompted.

ISSUES WITH PRINTING IN 331:

A recurring issue is that the name of the printer changes in firmware
each time the printer gets a new IP address from DHCP.  

Your instructor knows how to repair this issue, so if your print jobs
fails, please report the issue ASAP.

Your failed job may prevent other cadets from printing from their 
computers. If you send something to the printer and it does not print,
the issue might be with you and not the printer.  If your print job 
fails, please be courteous and clear the print spooler.  

CLEARING THE PRINT SPOOLER:

1.  Click the "Start" button on the lower left corner of your screen.
2.  Find and click "settings."
3.  Click "Bluetooth & devices."
4.  Click "Printers & Scanners.
5.  Scroll down to the printer and click it.
6.  Open the print queue and delete jobs associated with your name.
